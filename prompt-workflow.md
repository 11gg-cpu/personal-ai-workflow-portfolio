# Prompt Workflow

## My Standard Pattern

1. Define the role and output format.
2. Provide context and constraints.
3. Ask for assumptions and missing information.
4. Generate a first draft or plan.
5. Check facts, numbers, tone, and feasibility.
6. Iterate into a final deliverable.

## Validation Rules

- Do not publish numbers that were not checked.
- Do not claim skills or achievements that are not real.
- Keep prompts and outputs aligned with the target audience.
